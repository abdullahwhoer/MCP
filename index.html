About Us

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

The Muslim College Peshawar has been a beacon of knowledge and academic excellence for decades. Our mission is to nurture intellectual growth and moral values in our students, preparing them to be the leaders of tomorrow.

Our vision is to provide world-class education with a focus on holistic development, critical thinking, and character building in a supportive and inclusive environment.
